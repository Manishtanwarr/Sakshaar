About RIASEC Theory

What is RIASEC?

The RIASEC model was developed by psychologist John L. Holland and is one of the most widely used career assessment tools. It categorizes people into six personality types based on their interests and preferences:

R

Realistic

People who like to work with their hands, tools, and machines. They prefer practical, hands-on activities.

I

Investigative

People who enjoy research, analysis, and solving complex problems. They like to think and observe.

A

Artistic

People who enjoy creative expression and original thinking. They value aesthetics and creativity.

S

Social

People who enjoy helping, teaching, and working with others. They prefer cooperative environments.

E

Enterprising

People who enjoy leadership, business, and persuading others. They like to take risks and make decisions.

C

Conventional

People who prefer organized, structured work with clear procedures and attention to detail.
